Company's History

QCS which is know as Qolane Construction Service was establish in 2018 by Mr Qolane Eric and assisted

by Mr William Shao

This business began in Leribe district Lesotho

Qolane's Construction Service since became one of the best service business that was establish

during the heavy storms .

The aim of starting this business was to provide services to people who are in need

with the lowest price.Eventhough it was tricky to begin this business,

we knew the mission would be accomplished by customers help.

The business began by building homes for customers and

beautifying their floors with tiles.

Then more customers knew about our services ,they

became many until the company decides to have branches in different districts.

In 2021, many people became interested in this company from different countries,

that is where Mr Eric began to sell shares .

The most important objective of this company was to fulfil the customers needs.
